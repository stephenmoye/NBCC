Living Nativity

December 18th-20th

Visit Staff Contact

New Beginnings Community Church invites everyone to come and witness a live re-enactment of Jesus’ birth. This spectacular evening takes you on a guided tour through the events surrounding the birth of our Savior, Jesus Christ. Save the date, invite family and friends, and be ready to go back in history to celebrate the true Reason for the Christmas season, Jesus.


Shows will be held on:
Friday, December 18th
Saturday, December 19th
Sunday, December 20th

All showtimes begin at 6pm with groups running every 15 minutes up until 9pm.
