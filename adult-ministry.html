JOURNEYMEN MEN'S MINISTRY


Men of God live with the purpose to reign as leaders by utilizing mercy and selflessness, to love others as God does, and to fight the daily spiritual battles for the ones we care for. Our activities empower men to live according to God's calling. Join us as we become Men of Purpose together.
