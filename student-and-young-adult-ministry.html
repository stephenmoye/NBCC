Converge Capacity
From 6th Grade to Young Adult, we're focused on the next generation to arise in the world and lead lives for God.
C N V R G
Designed for 6th grade through Jr. High students, Converge is a dynamic, fun, and energetic environment to connect with the God and our ministry. We create a fun atmosphere with games, and live music with bible lessons that are relevant to our youth.

CAPACITY

Engage in an environment designed for High School students and up. Here you won't be judged, but have an opportunity to connect, be refined, and interact with your friends and leaders.
