We’re here to help!

To live a haphazard life of chance and indecision is to choose powerlessness and to give control of our behavior and decisions to others, thereby forfeiting the power and freedom that God’s grace, truth, and forgiveness grants us! New Beginnings believes that the church should serve a function similar to that of a hospital, moving alongside others, down the road to a healthier life. Many of us come from a painful past that has a firm grip on our present. These unhealthy roots spring up in our marriages, families and other relationships along our path in life. I want to encourage all of you. There is good news? We don’t have to face the future alone and we can change the way we do things. We have a wide variety of groups and classes to help you grow relationships God’s way. We hope to assist you in your move toward a healthier tomorrow.



Eric A. Disney, M.S.

Pastor, Marriage, Family and Relationship Ministry

RELATIONSHIP ENHANCEMENT


Boundaries

This class is designed to help you develop clear boundaries which are essential to a healthy, balanced lifestyle. You will develop insights into how to set healthy boundaries with our parents, spouses, children, friends, co-workers, and even ourselves – boundaries that give us freedom to walk as the loving, giving, fulfilled individuals God created us to be. (Co-ed) Cost: $25 (resources included) Times: Thursday Nights 7p - 8:45p Dates: Jan. 12 - Mar. 30

The Five Love Languages

If you express love in a way your spouse doesn’t understand, he or she won’t realize you’ve expressed your love at all. This study is designed to help you work as a team. Before long, you’ll learn to speak and understand the unique languages of love and effectively express your love as well as feel truly loved in return. (Co-ed) Cost: $20 (resources included) Times: Thursday Nights 7p – 8:45p Dates: Apr. 4 - May 18

Managing Our Anger

Life is full of frustrations – some big, some little. And while you might not be ready to blow, it’s easy to grow angry in the wake of disappointment, hurt, rejection, and embarrassment. Anger is a reality of life but it doesn’t have to control your life. Anger can be used for good and even change the world! You’ll develop insights into why you get angry and what you can do about it as you learn to control this powerful emotion rather than allowing it to control you. (Co-ed) Cost: $20 (resources included) Times: Thursday Nights 7p – 8:45p Dates: Jul. 27 – Sep. 14 Couple’s Date Night Dates: TBA

MARRIAGE & FAMILY

Marriage by Design

Teaches foundational techniques that will greatly enrich your marriage, regardless the degree of distress, as we learn God’s definition of a successful marriage based on selflessness. This is a venture in utilizing God’s design in marriage to re-establish a healthier foundation and undo some of the damage that has occurred through our marital journeys. MBD demonstrates that even with all the challenges that couples face today, marital harmony need not be considered an impossible ideal. (For traditional married couples.) Cost: $40/couple (resources included) Times: Thursday Nights 7p – 8:45p Winter: Jan. 12 - Mar. 30 Spring: Mar. 2 – May 18 Summer: Jul. 27 – Oct. 12 Fall: Aug. 31 – Nov. 16

Preparing For Marriage

Marriage is an opportunity to learn how to love, a gift and refining process. We define a successful marriage as we explore ourselves and each other and learn how to bring a healthy self into our lifelong commitment. (For traditional couples and required for marriage by a NBCC pastor.) Cost: $70/Couple (resources included) Times: Sunday Mornings 8:30a – 10:30a Winter: Feb. 27 – Mar. 26 Summer: Jul. 9 – Aug. 6 Fall: Oct. 15 – Nov. 12
